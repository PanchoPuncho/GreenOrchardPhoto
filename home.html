About Us

GOP is here to provide our customers with a high quality service at an unbeatable price!

GOP is owned and run by brothers Marco Mendoza & Rafael Mendoza. Rafael started shooting when he was 9 and has been passionate about capturing special moments ever since. GOP is committed to provide professional quality services at an affordable price. GOP’s vision is to expand nationally. Due to this, they will cover any major event or commercial work anywhere in the state of Washington. Call Green Orchard Productions if you need any type of portrait, commercial work, or event covered.

Non-profit, for the community, & charity events qualify for reduced coverage!

How We Work

Our Process

Here is an overview of how we approach each new project.

Plan

  • Client Interview
  • Create a Shared Vision
  • Arrange Schedule and Location
  • Sign Contract and Pay Deposit

Shoot

  • Review Shared Vision
  • Receive Remaining Balance
  • Take Photographs
  • Answer Additional Questions

Edit

  • Review Images Thoroughly
  • Discard Flawed Images
  • Edit Each Picture Individually
  • Deliver Hi-Resolution Product
